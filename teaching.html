Teaching

I have ten years of experience and training in teaching since the beginning of my career. I am convinced that an engaged student will learn and retain the material of a class more efficiently. During the Covid-19 crisis, this belief became even more important in my teaching style, and I realized that it was my responsibility to create an environment in which students want to be involved.
