	<h2>About me</h2>

	<p>
		My name is Pierre-Olivier Parisé. Since Fall 2024, I am an assistant professor (tenure track position) at the <a href="https://oraprdnt.uqtr.uquebec.ca/portail/gscw031?owa_no_site=1645" class="aLink" title="Dep. Math UQTR French" target="_blank">Université du Quebec à Trois-Rivières</a>. 
	</p>

	<p>
		Previously, I was a temporary assistant professor at the <a href="https://math.hawaii.edu/wordpress/" class = "aLink" title = "Dep. Math. UH" target="blank_">Department of Mathematics of the University of Hawaii at Manoa</a>, working alongside <a href=https://math.hawaii.edu/~myounsi/ target=_blank class=aLink title="Malik Younsi">Malik Younsi</a>. My research was supported by a postdoctoral research schorlarship (B3X) and an NSERC postdoctoral scholarship.
	</p>

	<p>
		I received my Ph. D. from Laval University (QC, Ca). My supervisor was <a href="https://www.mat.ulaval.ca/departement-et-professeurs/direction-personnel-et-etudiants/professeurs/fiche-de-professeur/show/ransford-thomas" class="aLink" title="Thomas J. Ransford" target="blank_">Thomas J. Ransford</a>. My research was funded by the Alexander-Graham Bell Graduate scholarship from NSERC and the graduate scholarship from the FRQNT.
	</p>

	<p>
		I obtained a Masters' degree in 2017 from Université du Québec à Trois-Rivières. My supervisor was <a href = "http://www.3dfractals.com/" title="Dominic Rochon" class="aLink" target="blank_">Dominic Rochon</a>. My research was funded by the Alexander-Graham Bell Graduate scholarship from the NSERC and the scholarship for graduate programs from the FRQNT.
	</p>

	<p>
		My favorite hobbies mostly take place in the water. I really enjoy swimming, paddleboarding, surfing, and wing foiling. Whenever I can, I go surf in my hometown, Sept-Îles. I also enjoy climbing, especially bouldering and playing hockey and soccer.
	</p>
