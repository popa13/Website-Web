Lecture Notes

You will find in the tables below the lecture notes for each section of each chapter. You may print it and fill them out during lectures. If you have a tablet, you can bring your tablet and fill them out on your tablet.

The lecture notes are based on the book Calculus, 8th Edition by James Stewart. All the exercises suggested in the tables below will be found in this book.

Chapter 1

In the first chapter, we will see the notion of limit and continuous functions. But before that, we will go through some concepts that you've already seen in pre-calculus.

The answers to the odd numbers are in Appendix H of the textbook.

Chapter 4

In the fourth chapter, we will introduct the integral of a function. We will see how the integral of a function is related to the area under the graph of a positive function. We will also see that the integral is sort of the inverse operation of the derivative through the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus.

The answers to the odd numbers are in Appendix H of the textbook.
