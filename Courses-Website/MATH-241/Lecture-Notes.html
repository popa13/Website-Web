You will find in the tables below the lecture notes for each section of each chapter. You may print it and fill them out during lectures. If you have a tablet, you can bring your tablet and fill them out with your tablet.

The lecture notes are based on the book Calculus, 8th Edition by James Stewart. All the exercises suggested in the tables below will be found in this book.

Chapter 4

In the fourth chapter, we will introduce the integral of a function. We will see how the integral of a function is related to the area under the graph of a positive function. We will also introduce the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus and how it can be used to compute the exact value of an integral.

The answers to the odd numbers are in the Appendix H of the textbook.
