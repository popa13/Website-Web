Instructions

Please follow these instructions to format and to upload your homework.

Format

Follow these rules to format your homework:

  • Write down the question number clearly.
  • After the question number, write down your answer to the multiple choice question.
  • Make sure your solutions to the problem are in the order they are presented in the assignment.
Here is a valid example:
Question 1: a)
Question 2: b)
Question 3: d)
Question 4: e)
Question 5: b)

If you submit a homework that meets the above template, then you will receive a bonus of 5% for this homework.

The first homework is an exception to the above rules. You will have to submit the three PDFs on Laulima. See the instructions below to submit your PDFs.

Upload on Laulima

Follow these instructions to upload the first homework on Laulima.

  • Your file must be in a PDF Format. No other format will be accepted.
  • Your file's name must be LASTNAMEFIRSTNAME_HWX.pdf where X is the homework number. No other name will be accepted.
  • Depending on the device you used to complete your homework:
    • Handwritten homework: Scan your homework with a good scanner. Verify the quality of your documents before submitting it to Laulima. If the scanned document is poor quality, it won't be accepted.
    • Tablet: Export your homework in a PDF file. Verify that everything is in the right place before submitting to Laulima.
  • Your homework must be uploaded on Laulima.

Below are examples of a correct file's name and an incorrect file's name:

  • Correct file's name for homework 1: ParisePierreOlivier_HW1.pdf.
  • Incorrect file's name for homework 1: HW1.pdf.
