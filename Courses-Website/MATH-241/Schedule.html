Schedule

In the table you see, you will find important information on the schedule of the course. In each line, you will find respectively

  • the number of the week;
  • what we will do during Monday, Wednesday, and Friday of this week;
  • the lecture notes for each week.
The problems to do for the week are written in the lecture notes.

Legend

  • A: Assigned
  • DD: Date Due (homework).
  • HW: Homework
  • WS: Worksheet
  • MT: Midterm
  • FE: Final Exam
  • Rec: Recitation
  • ---: No lecture
