Lecture Notes

You will find in the tables below the lecture notes for each section of each chapter. You may print it and fill them out during lectures. If you have a tablet, you can bring your tablet and fill them out on your tablet.

The lecture notes are based on the book Linear Algebra and Differential Equations by Gary L. Peterson an James S. Sochacki. All the exercises suggested in the tables below will be found in this book. The answers to the odd numbers are in the appendix, p. 441.

I encourage you to share a book with your collegues or to get a used textbook. Books can be expensive and the content of the course are basically all in my lecture notes.
