Instructions

Please follow these instructions to format and to upload your homework.

Format

Follow these rules to format your homework:

  • Write down your complete name (First and Last Name) in the right corner of the first page of your homework.
  • Write down the homework number in the left corner of the first page of your homework.
  • Write down clearly the question number you are answering.
  • Turn in your solutions to the problem in the order they are presented in the tables above.
  • Write down one question per page.
  • Number each page.
  • Optional: Specify the section.
Here is an example: Homework 1 format example.

Upload

Follow these instructions to upload your homework on Laulima (see the Assignment section in the left menu of Laulima website).

  • Your file must be in a PDF Format. No other format will be accepted.
  • Your file's name must be LASTNAMEFIRSTNAME_HWX.pdf where X is the homework number. No other name will be accepted.
  • Scan your homework with a good scanner. Verify the quality of your documents before submitting it to Laulima. If the scanned document is poor quality, it won't be accepted.

Below are examples of an incorrect file's name and a correct file name:

  • Correct file's name for homework 1: ParisePierreOlivier_HW1.pdf.
  • Incorrect file's name for homework 1: HW1.pdf.
