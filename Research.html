Research Interests

My main research interests are in the field of complex analysis and operator theory. My main interest is in spaces of holomorphic functions. I am also interested in holomorphic dynamics and its generalizations to higher dimensions using the multicomplex numbers.

Here are some of my research papers, academic work, and softwares related to my research.
